# DQA-Learning-Query-Agnostic-Black-Box-Attacks-on-LLM-Based-Retrieval

Our experiments are mainly conducted on the benchmark platform "BRIGHT":
https://github.com/xlang-ai/BRIGHT

To replicate our implementation, please: 

(1) Follow BRIGHT's instruction to install the environment and datasets;

(2) Replace the "run.py", "retrievers.py" and "tools.py" with files in this repository;

(3) By choosing the specific attack in the run.py and running desired setting, our experiment xould be replicated.
